Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

360ROI ("we", "us", "our") is operated by Scaletopeak Consulting. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website audit service.

Information We Collect

When you purchase an audit, we collect: your website URL, business type, CMS/platform, industry, phone number, and email address. Payment information is processed securely by Cashfree — we do not store card details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To perform the website audit you have paid for
  • To deliver your audit report to your email address
  • To send follow-up emails about your report, including Fix Guide upgrade information
  • To improve our audit models and benchmarking data (anonymised)

Data Storage

Audit data and reports are stored securely on Supabase infrastructure. We retain audit records for 12 months. You may request deletion at any time by emailing us.

Third-Party Services

We use: Google PageSpeed Insights API, Firecrawl, ScreenshotOne, Cashfree, and Gmail. Each operates under its own privacy policy.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at hello@360roi.ai.
